# Privacy Policy for "LockedIn" Google Extension

**Last Updated: May 4, 2026**

## Data Collection
This extension does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information stays on your device.

## Local Storage
- Your blocked/allowed website lists are stored locally in your browser
- Your preferences and settings are stored locally
- No data is ever sent to external servers

## Permissions
- **Storage**: Used to save your website lists locally
- **Tabs/WebNavigation**: Used to detect and block websites on your list
- **Host Permissions**: Required to block any website you choose to add

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.
